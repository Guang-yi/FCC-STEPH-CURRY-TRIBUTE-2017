Stephen Curry

MVP

Hardwork pays off

He isn't particularly tall or athletic to be an NBA player. When you look at Steph Curry, the first thing that comes to mind might just be that "hey, he looks kind of like a kid amongst men". And honestly, that's why I appreciate Steph so much as a player. He epitomizes the principles of hardwork and perseverence, and I look to him as a lesson on how to achieve true greatness.

  • 1988 - Born in Akron, Ohio to Dell Curry.
  • Played college at Davidson College. He was deemed to small to play at the major schools.
  • Drafted in 2009 by the Golden State Warriors, and eventually developed into one of pro basketball's top players with his stellar shooting skills.
  • Led the Golden State Warriors to the NBA championship in 2015. League leading 73 wins.

"Make it work no matter what you have to work with - that's something that stuck with me very early on as a point guard. Adjust. Get Creative. Try a different angle, a different lane, a different move or a different shot - just make it work." -Stephen Curry
